# Phonton CLI v0.12.6

v0.12.6 hardens the provider reliability work from v0.12.5.

## Fixes

- DeepSeek now has a first-class provider identity in diagnostics instead of
  showing up as anonymous `openai-compatible`.
- DeepSeek V4 and reasoner routes disable provider thinking for diff-only
  worker calls, avoiding responses that contain reasoning but no final
  `message.content`.
- Provider-health cache keys are versioned, so stale v0.12.5 canary passes are
  ignored and routes are re-tested under the corrected request contract.
- Reasoning-only OpenAI-compatible replies now fail with a clear provider
  contract error.
- Settings and provider-doctor canaries time out after 20 seconds.
