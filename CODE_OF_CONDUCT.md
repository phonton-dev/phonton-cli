# Code Of Conduct

Phonton is for serious engineering work. Keep project discussion direct, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be factual and specific.
- Critique code and claims, not people.
- Assume good intent, but ask for evidence when claims affect users.
- Keep issues and PRs focused.
- Respect privacy and do not post secrets, private code, or private provider responses.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Spam or low-effort promotional content.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or restrict participation when behavior harms the project.

Report problems privately to the maintainers.
